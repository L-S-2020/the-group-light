# The group light
<img src="https://github.com/L-S-2020/the-group-light/blob/main/readme_pictures/front.jpeg?raw=true" alt="the console" width="800"/>

I recently stumbled across the PrintU tool on [MakerLab](https://makerworld.com/de/makerlab), which creates 3d printables figures from a photo. I decided to try it and made a birthday present out of it. The lamp runs Wled, so it has plenty of animations to play with.

My friend Jan took over the wood working part and I did the electronics and cad designs.

You can view the cad design on onshape: https://cad.onshape.com/documents/962c9a80c1e4ac822a06726f/w/4a66812e610f37dfee0943a3/e/304eb1700773e5976a82c6aa?renderMode=0&uiState=69bc8ff95d4f32291894b696

## Parts
- [ESP32 devboard](https://www.amazon.de/dp/B071P98VTG)
- 7x [WS2812 Led ring](https://www.amazon.de/dp/B0BZDQQY6B)
- 3d printer + white PLA
- wooden board
